Space Schedule for Astronauts

Week of Launch

  • Monday: Launch to the International Space Station
  • Tuesday: Docking with the ISS and safety briefing
  • Wednesday: Installation of new equipment on the spacecraft!
  • Thursday: Spacewalk maintenance work
  • Friday: Scientific experiments and research
  • Saturday: Day off for crew rest and relaxation
  • Sunday: Preparing for return trip to Earth
