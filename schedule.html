Schedule Timeline!

    Training Week


  • DAY 1: Health/Fitness check and Meet your tour guide

  • DAY 2: Tour of Ship and space suit fitting

  • DAY 3: Zero Gravity Simulation Training

  • DAY 4: Crash Course on Space aerodynamics and science

  • DAY 6: Introduction to living in outerspace (Astronaut food and hygeine)

  • DAY 7: Final check before launch week

    Week of Launch


  • DAY 1: Launch to the International Space Station

  • DAY 2: Docking with the ISS and safety briefing

  • DAY 3: Installation of new equipment on the spacecraft!

  • DAY 4: Spacewalk maintenance work

  • DAY 5: Scientific experiments and research

  • DAY 6: Day off for crew rest and relaxation

  • DAY 7: Preparing for return trip to Earth
