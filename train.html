Space Flights to Tommorowland

Training

Anyone interested in going on tour will have to:

  • Pass a background check
  • Learn how to handle zero gravity
  • Endure high intensity workouts
  • Go through space flight simulations

    • For your Health and Safety:

  • You will be provided a fully trained and licensed astronaut as your tour guide
  • You must pass a medical exam to determine your fitness level
  • Sign a waiver stating we are not responsible for anything you may experience on your tour
  • Get accustomed to eating astronaut food

    • All forms and training must be completed 1 week before your tour
