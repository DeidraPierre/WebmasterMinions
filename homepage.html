Featured destination: Saturn

  • Saturn is the second largest planet in the solar system. This planet is surrounded by luxurious black and gold rings made of massive pieces of ice, varying in all types of shapes and sizes. Although the exact age of this planet is unknown, it is estimated to be approximately 4.5 billion years old. Saturn is a visual spectacle but also a scientific paradise. Due to its physical makeup of hydrogen and helium, Saturn has many scientific discoveries yet to be uncovered. Though many things are still unknown, Saturn’s beauty is undeniably prominent. A visit to Saturn is the visit of a lifetime. ​
